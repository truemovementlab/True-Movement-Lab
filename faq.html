FAQs

If you’re unsure, submit the questionnaire and we’ll sort it out together.

Who is True Movement Lab coaching for?

This coaching is for people who love to explore the world through movement — any age, any skill level. We typically work with parkour, bouldering/climbing, hiking, mountain biking, trail/ultra running, and obstacle course racing. If your sport isn’t listed, we can still build a plan that fits.

Do I need a gym?

Having access to a gym or basic equipment is strongly recommended, but not required. We can work around your circumstances and build a plan using what you’ve got.

Can you program around injuries or pain?

Yes. We manage range, volume, exercise selection, and progressive re-loading. If something needs a medical opinion, we’ll recommend the right professional and program around that guidance.

How long until I see results?

Most people feel early wins within 2–4 weeks (structure, recovery, consistency). Meaningful strength and capacity changes are usually 6–12 weeks. Durability compounds over months.

Can I cancel or pause?

Yes — you can pause for up to 6 weeks for holidays/recovery/life. If you stop, you’re always welcome back with no sign-up fees.
